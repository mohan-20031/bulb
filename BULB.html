ABOUT BULB:

*An electronic bulb is a small and simple light source that uses a wire filament to glow on the application of electricity.
*The structure of incandescent light bulbs is shown in the figure below.
*The filament, which is a coiled thin wire, is made of tungsten.


TYPES OF BULB:

  • Fluorescent.
  • Light-Emitting Diode (LED) Bulbs.
  • Compact Fluorescent (CFL) Bulbs.
  • Sodium Vapor Lamps.
  • Mercury Vapor Lamps.
  • Smart Lightbulbs.

ADVANTAGES:

  1. Bulbs offer longer life than any other light source.
  2. The working time ranges from 8000 to 15000 hours.
  3. It is affordable and economical.
  4. It is easy to install.
  5. It comes in various sizes and shapes.
  6. Produces relatively high output.

DISADVANTAGES:

  1. It is energy inefficient.
  2. It produces warm light.
  3. A short lamp lifetime is about 1000 hours typically.
  4. Requires higher operating cost.
  5. Bulb generates low lumen per watt.
